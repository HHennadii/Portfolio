003 : Reborn Ebro brend

Blender 3D / UVlayout / Marmoset Toolbag / Substance Painter / Photoshop

The most significant case in my professional career. As part of this project, I created a unique design for the front of the car, which eventually became a reality. I was invited to join a small development team with the goal of building an electric pickup truck under the brand name of an old Spanish car company. In a very short time and with great technical limitations, the task was to create the exterior of the car. In addition to the design itself, it was required to create a series of visualizations for use in promotional materials, a series of videos, and an AR ready model. This project was huge with a lot of skill needed from me as I was in the position of the lead 3D designer. Below you can see some of the visual material and photos of a real prototype based on the created design. You can also visit the Ebro project website.
