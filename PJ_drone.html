moDel

My interaction with Modern Expo contains many cases, one of them is the design for the last mile direction. As part of this project, I was doing full visual design under the supervision of lead designer Modrn. The video sequence presents a drone project close to implementation. As part of this project, I was given a technical model of the drone and tasked with developing a suitable both functionally and visually design solution. As a final showcase, I created the following video.

Mebius

Mebius, a deeply conceptual solution to the last mile delivery problem, was developed in a collaboration with the Modrn lead designer for the Euroshop 2020 exhibition. As part of this project, the task was to find a design solution for last mile delivery, after reviewing many options, we chose the closest concept to the company's products. The main idea was to create a platform with modular parcel lockers, which worked as a recharging station for drones. Also, this complex has its own wheeled drones to deliver goods to the doorstep. As a result of this project, a scale physical model was created, which was shown at the exhibition, as well as the video below.
